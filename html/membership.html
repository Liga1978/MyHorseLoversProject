  1. Horses are sleeping standing up;
  2. Horses Can't Burp;
  3. You Can Estimate a Horse's Age by Its Teeth;
  4. Horses Can Live to Be More Than 30 Years Old;
  5. The American Quarter Horse Is the World's Most Popular Breed;
  6. (Most) Arabian Horses Have One Less Vertebrae Than Other Breeds;
  7. Horses Are Herd Animals;
  8. Horses Were Domesticated by Humans More Than 3,000 Years Ago;
  9. Horses Are Measured in "Hands";
  10. Horses Are Not Native to North America;
  11. The Original "Horse" Was the Size of a Golden Retriever;
